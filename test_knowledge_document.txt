AutoBoss Machine Troubleshooting Guide

This is a test knowledge document for the AI Assistant system.

Common Issues:
1. Machine not starting - Check power connection and fuses
2. Low pressure - Inspect hydraulic fluid levels and connections
3. Unusual noises - Check for loose components or worn parts

Maintenance Schedule:
- Daily: Check fluid levels
- Weekly: Inspect connections and hoses
- Monthly: Replace filters and check pressure settings

Safety Warnings:
- Always disconnect power before maintenance
- Use proper PPE when working with hydraulic systems
- Never exceed recommended pressure limits
