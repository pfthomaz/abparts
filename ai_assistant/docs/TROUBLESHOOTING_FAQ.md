## Chat Interface Issues

### Q: The chat icon doesn't appear
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page** - Press F5 or Ctrl+R (Cmd+R on Mac)
2. **Clear browser cache** - Clear your browser's cache and cookies
3. **Check browser compatibility** - Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
4. **Disable ad blockers** - Some ad blockers may hide the chat icon
5. **Check your login** - Ensure you're logged into ABParts

### Q: The chat window won't open
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Click directly on the icon** - Make sure you're clicking the chat icon itself
2. **Wait a moment** - The first load may take a few seconds
3. **Check JavaScript** - Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
4. **Try a different browser** - Test with another browser to isolate the issue

### Q: My messages aren't sending
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Check internet connection** - Verify you have active internet connectivity
2. **Look for error messages** - Read any error messages that appear
3. **Try typing again** - Sometimes a simple retry works
4. **Refresh the page** - Reload ABParts and try again
5. **Check message length** - Very long messages may need to be split up

### Q: I see "AI is typing..." but no response comes
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Wait 30-60 seconds** - AI responses can take time for complex questions
2. **Check your connection** - Slow internet may delay responses
3. **Refresh if stuck** - If stuck for >2 minutes, refresh and try again
4. **Simplify your question** - Try asking in a simpler, more direct way
5. **Report the issue** - Use the escalation feature if problems persist

**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Speak clearly** - Enunciate words clearly at normal pace
2. **Reduce background noise** - Move to a quieter location

### Q: Voice output doesn't play
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Check volume** - Ensure device volume is turned up
2. **Check mute status** - Unmute your device/browser

4. **Check browser permissions** - Ensure audio playback is allowed
5. **Refresh the page** - Reload and try again

### Q: Voice output is in the wrong language
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Check your profile language** - Go to ABParts profile settings
2. **Update language preference** - Change to your preferred language
3. **Refresh after changing** - Reload the page after language change
4. **Clear browser cache** - Old language settings may be cached

## Troubleshooting Workflow Issues

### Q: The assistant doesn't understand my problem
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Be more specific** - Provide detailed description of the issue
2. **Include symptoms** - Describe what you see, hear, or smell
3. **Mention when it started** - Note when the problem began
4. **Describe what changed** - Mention any recent changes or maintenance
5. **Use simple language** - Avoid jargon or overly technical terms

**Example of a good problem description:**
"My AutoBoss V4.0 machine started making a loud grinding noise during the cleaning cycle yesterday. It happens about 5 minutes into the cycle. We replaced the pump filter last week."

### Q: The troubleshooting steps don't match my machine
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Verify machine selection** - Confirm you selected the correct machine model
2. **Provide feedback** - Tell the assistant the steps don't apply
3. **Describe your machine** - Mention specific model details
4. **Request escalation** - Ask for human expert assistance

### Q: I completed all steps but the problem isn't resolved
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Review steps** - Double-check you completed each step correctly
2. **Provide detailed feedback** - Describe exactly what happened at each step
3. **Request escalation** - Ask to escalate to human support
4. **Try again later** - Some issues may need time to manifest
5. **Document the issue** - Take photos/videos to share with support

## Escalation and Support

### Q: How do I escalate to human support?
**A:** Tell the assistant "I need human support" or "Escalate this issue". The assistant will:
1. Compile your conversation history
2. Gather machine details and attempted solutions
3. Create a support ticket
4. Provide contact information for technical support

### Q: What information is included in an escalation?
**A:** The escalation includes:
- Complete conversation history
- Machine model, serial number, and details
- All troubleshooting steps attempted
- Your responses and observations
- Relevant maintenance history
- Timestamp and user information

### Q: How long does it take to get human support?
**A:** Response times vary by support level:
- **Standard Support**: Within 24 hours
- **Priority Support**: Within 4 hours
- **Emergency Support**: Within 1 hour (for critical issues)

Check your support agreement for specific response times.

## Privacy and Security

### Q: Is my conversation private?
**A:** Yes. All conversations are:
- Encrypted during transmission
- Stored securely
- Accessible only to you and authorized support staff
- Subject to data privacy regulations (GDPR, etc.)

### Q: Can I delete my conversation history?
**A:** Yes. You can:
1. Request deletion through your ABParts profile settings
2. Contact your administrator
3. Submit a data deletion request per GDPR rights

### Q: What data does the AI Assistant collect?
**A:** The assistant collects:
- Conversation messages
- Machine selection and context
- Troubleshooting outcomes
- Usage analytics (anonymized)
- Voice input (processed, not stored)

### Q: Is my voice data stored?
**A:** No. Voice input is:
- Converted to text in real-time
- Not recorded or stored as audio
- Processed securely
- Deleted after conversion

## Performance and Reliability

### Q: Why are responses slow?
**Possible Causes:**
1. **Complex questions** - Detailed analysis takes more time
2. **Slow internet** - Check your connection speed
3. **High system load** - Peak usage times may be slower
4. **Large knowledge base search** - Comprehensive searches take time

**Typical Response Times:**
- Simple questions: 2-5 seconds
- Troubleshooting analysis: 5-15 seconds
- Complex diagnostics: 15-30 seconds

### Q: The assistant seems to be offline
**Possible Solutions:**
1. **Check internet connection** - Verify connectivity
2. **Check system status** - Look for maintenance notifications
3. **Try again later** - System may be temporarily unavailable
4. **Use alternative support** - Contact support directly if urgent

### Q: Can I use the assistant offline?
**A:** No. The AI Assistant requires internet connectivity to:
- Access the knowledge base
- Process natural language
- Retrieve machine information
- Generate responses

## Tips for Best Results

### Writing Effective Messages

**DO:**
- Be specific and detailed
- Describe symptoms clearly
- Mention when the problem started
- Include relevant context
- Use complete sentences

**DON'T:**
- Use vague descriptions ("it's broken")
- Skip important details
- Use excessive jargon
- Send one-word messages
- Expect mind-reading

### Getting Accurate Troubleshooting

**Best Practices:**
1. **Select the correct machine** - Always choose the right machine
2. **Follow steps in order** - Don't skip ahead
3. **Report results accurately** - Be honest about what you observe
4. **Ask for clarification** - If unsure, ask before proceeding
5. **Take your time** - Rushing leads to mistakes

### Using Voice Effectively

**Voice Input Tips:**
- Speak at normal conversational pace
- Use short, clear sentences
- Minimize background noise
- Pause between thoughts
- Repeat if not recognized correctly

**Voice Output Tips:**
- Adjust volume before starting
- Use headphones in noisy environments
- Pause playback to take notes
- Replay if you missed something
