## Getting Started

### Accessing the AI Assistant

1. **Log in to ABParts** with your credentials
2. **Look for the Chat Icon** in the bottom-right corner of any page
3. **Click to Open** the AI Assistant chat window
4. **Start Chatting** - describe your issue or ask questions

### First-Time Setup

The AI Assistant automatically:
- Detects your preferred language from your ABParts profile
- Accesses your organization's machines and maintenance history
- Remembers your conversation history during your session

No additional setup required!

## Using the Chat Interface

### Basic Chat Features

**Opening and Closing**
- Click the chat icon to open the assistant
- Click the X button or icon again to close
- Your conversation is saved even when closed

**Message Input**
- Type your message in the text box
- Press Enter or click Send to submit
- Send multiple messages in a row if needed

### Starting a Troubleshooting Session

**Describe Your Problem Clearly**

Good examples:
- "My AutoBoss V4.0 machine won't start"
- "The HP gauge is showing unusual readings"
- "Water is leaking from the bottom of the machine"
- "The cleaning cycle stops halfway through"

**Select Your Machine**

When troubleshooting, the assistant will ask you to select your machine:
1. Choose from your organization's registered machines
2. The assistant loads machine-specific information
3. Guidance is tailored to your specific model

### Following Troubleshooting Steps

The AI Assistant provides **step-by-step guidance**:

1. **Read Each Step**: Clear, actionable instructions
2. **Perform the Action**: Follow the instruction on your machine
3. **Report Results**: Tell the assistant what happened
4. **Continue**: Next steps adapt based on your feedback
