# User Guide: Adding Photos and Organization Logos

## üéØ Overview

You can now personalize ABParts by adding:
- **Your profile photo** - Shows in the header and menus

## üë§ How to Add Your Profile Photo

### Step 1: Go to Your Profile
1. Click on your **user icon** in the top-right corner of the screen
2. Click **"My Profile"** from the dropdown menu

### Step 2: Upload Your Photo
1. At the top of the profile page, you'll see a **"Profile Photo"** section
2. You'll see either:
   - Your current photo (if you've already uploaded one)
   - A placeholder circle with a "?" 
3. Click the **"Upload Photo"** button
4. Select an image from your computer
5. Wait a moment for the upload to complete

### Step 3: See Your Photo
- Your photo will immediately appear in the header (top-right corner)
- It will also show in the user dropdown menu
- It replaces the colored circle with your initials

### To Remove Your Photo
1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Click the **"Remove Photo"** button
3. Your photo will be removed and the initials circle will return

---

## üè¢ How to Add Your Organization Logo (Admins Only)

**Note:** Only organization administrators can upload organization logos.

### Step 1: Go to Organizations
1. Click on **"Organizations"** in the navigation menu
2. Find your organization in the list

### Step 2: Edit Your Organization
1. Click the **"Edit"** button on your organization card
2. A form will open

### Step 3: Upload the Logo
1. At the top of the form, you'll see an **"Organization Logo"** section
2. You'll see either:
   - Your current logo (if one exists)
   - A "No Logo" placeholder
3. Click the **"Upload Logo"** button
4. Select an image from your computer (preferably a square logo or transparent PNG)
5. Wait for the upload to complete

### Step 4: Save and See Your Logo
1. The logo will be uploaded immediately
2. You can continue editing other organization details if needed
3. Click **"Save"** or **"Cancel"** to close the form
4. Your logo will now appear next to your organization name in the header!

### To Remove the Logo
1. Go to **Organizations** > **Edit** your organization
2. Click the **"Remove Logo"** button
3. The logo will be removed

---

## üì∏ Image Requirements

### Profile Photos
- **File types:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
- **Max size:** 5MB
- **Recommended:** Square images, at least 200x200 pixels
- **Display:** Shown as a circular avatar

### Organization Logos
- **File types:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
- **Max size:** 5MB
- **Recommended:** 
  - Square logos work best
  - Transparent PNG files look professional
  - Simple, clear designs are easier to see at small sizes
- **Display:** Shown as a small icon next to the organization name

## üí° Tips for Best Results

### For Profile Photos:
- Use a clear, well-lit photo of yourself
- Face the camera directly
- Avoid busy backgrounds
- Square photos work best (they won't be cropped)

### For Organization Logos:
- Use your official company logo
- Ensure the logo is clear and readable at small sizes
- Transparent backgrounds look more professional
- Test how it looks in both light and dark themes

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "Upload Photo" button doesn't work
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the file is under 5MB
- Try a different image format (PNG or JPG)
- Refresh the page and try again

### Photo doesn't appear after upload
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- Clear your browser cache
- Log out and log back in

### Can't see "Upload Logo" button
- Make sure you're an **admin** of your organization
- Only admins can upload organization logos
- Regular users can only upload their own profile photos

### Logo looks blurry or pixelated
- Upload a higher resolution image
- Make sure the original image is at least 200x200 pixels
- Try a PNG file instead of JPG for better quality
