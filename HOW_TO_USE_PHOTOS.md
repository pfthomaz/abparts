## âœ… Upload Organization Logo (Admins Only)

1. Go to the Organizations page (from the navigation menu)
2. Find your organization and click "Edit"
3. You'll see an "Organization Logo" section at the top of the form
4. Click "Upload Logo" and select an image
5. The logo will appear next to your organization name in the header!

## â“ Troubleshooting

**Organization name not showing in header?**
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

**Can't see upload button?**
- For profile photos: Any user can upload their own photo
- For organization logos: Only admins can upload logos for their organization

**Photo not updating after upload?**
- Refresh the page to see the changes
- Clear your browser cache if needed
